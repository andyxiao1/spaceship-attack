You are a spaceship (Red Block)
Your goal is to complete the levels and get as many coins as possible
Make sure you still have health
Move with the arrow keys (Up, Down, Left, Right)
Shoot Asteroids and Enemy Ships with the Space Bar
Asteroids (Black Circles) do damage on collision
Enemy Ships (Blue Ovals) do damage on collision and shoot lasers
CAUTION: Asteroids and Enemy Ships get stronger in level 3
Highscores are determined by the most number of coins on level 3
